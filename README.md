#E-Commerce Customer Behaviour Dashboard
1. Project Title:
E-Commerce Customer Behaviour Dashboard

2. Short Description / Purpose:
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyse and visualise e-commerce customer behaviour data. It consolidates customer demographic, transactional, and engagement information into a unified data model which enabling at-a-glance monitoring of spending patterns, membership trends, city-level performance, satisfaction levels, and discount behaviour across the customer base.

3. Tech Stack:
. Power BI (Power BI Template / .pbit)
. Power Query – data ingestion & shaping
. DAX – measures and calculated columns (including Age Group segmentation)
. Data Modelling – relationships established across tables to enable cross-filtering and aggregation

4. Data Source: E-Commerce Customer Behaviour Dataset, and Fields include: Customer ID, Age, Gender, City, Membership Type, Total Spend, Items Purchased, Average Rating, Discount Applied, Days Since Last Purchase, Satisfaction Level with Format: Flat table, imported and shaped via Power Query, and Refresh: Static dataset loaded on report open; no live API or scheduled incremental refresh.

5. Features / Highlights:
.  Customer segmentation — Age Group calculated column categorises customers into Under 18, 18–25, 26–35, 36–45, and 46+ bands for demographic analysis.
. Spending analysis — Total spend broken down by city and membership type (Gold, Silver, Bronze)
. City-level performance — Side-by-side comparison of customer spend across San Francisco, New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Chicago, and Houston
. Membership insights — Visual breakdown of revenue contribution by membership tier
. Gender distribution — Donut chart showing male/female split across the customer base
. Satisfaction & ratings — Customer satisfaction level and average rating tracked across segments
. Discount impact — Analysis of discount applied vs spending behaviour

6. Key Visuals:
. Bar charts — Total Spend by City & Total Spend by Membership Type
. Donut chart — Gender distribution
. Segmentation visuals — Age Group, Satisfaction Level, Membership Type breakdowns
. KPI cards — Summary metrics across the dashboard
